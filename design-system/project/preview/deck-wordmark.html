Rules
Wordmark: Always top-center, ~10–12px height. wordmark-dark.svg on white at 35% opacity. wordmark-light.svg on dark at 25% opacity.
Top-left: Label or eyebrow — 11px, uppercase, wide tracking. Section name or topic identifier.
Top-right: Optional body text — 18px, regular weight, right-aligned. Provides secondary context.
Main zone: Hero headline, stat, or quote. Always the largest type. Positioned center or bottom-left.
Bottom-left: Section label — grounding context. "About Musketeers" or category name.
Bottom-right: Tagline or page reference. Never enlarge the wordmark — it stays top-center as a quiet brand anchor.
